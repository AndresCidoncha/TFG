Navigator

Closes AmigoSounds, then starts the Navigator application for intelligent control of AmigoBOT and other ActivMedia Robotics products.
 

Mapper

Disconnects and closes AmigoSounds, then starts the Mapper application for creating and editing maps for robot navigation.

Trainer

Disconnects and closes AmigoSounds, then starts the Trainer application for editing and executing your own robot control programs.
