Join ActivMedia Robot Users

ActivMedia Robotics sponsors email newslists dedicated to owners and users of Pioneer (PeopleBots, too) and AmigoBot Intelligent Mobile Robots. Each list -- pioneer-users and amigobot-users -- is a worldwide organization for people to share operations and programming tips and to get answers to their questions about their own robot, as well as other ActivMedia Robotics mobile robots.

You must first sign up to send and receive messages with AmigoBot- or Pioneer-Users:

To sign up:

Send email with your valid return email address
To: pioneer-users-requests@activmedia.com
Subject: subscribe
(notice the -requests on the end of the -users?)

Substitute amigo- for pioneer- to sign up for the AmigoBot Users list. You must sign up individually for each users group. Once signed up, you'll automatically receive messages from other AmigoBot or Pioneer users around the world.

To send a message to AmigoBot or Pioneer-Users:

Send email with the same return email address as you had signed up with,
To: amigobot-users@activmedia.com
Subject: Something of interest to AmigoBot Users
(notice there is no -requests at the end of the -users?)
Body: Please, only comments and questions of interest to other AmigoBot users. NO SPAM!

Substitute pioneer- for amigobot- to send messages to other Pioneer users.
